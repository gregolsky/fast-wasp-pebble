# FastWasp — Store Description

## Short tagline

Intermittent fasting on your wrist. With a sting. 🐝

---

## Long description

FastWasp is a free, native intermittent-fasting tracker for the new Pebble devices from Core Devices. Everything runs on the watch — no companion app, no phone, no accounts, no internet.

**6 fasting programs built in:**
- 12:12 — Worker Wasp
- 14:10 — Scouting Wasp
- 16:8 — Hunter Wasp
- 18:6 — Soldier Wasp
- 20:4 — Warrior Wasp
- OMAD — Warrior Queen

**Glance-first UI.** The main screen always shows a circular progress ring filling toward your target, a large HH:MM:SS countdown, and — once you pass the target — an **OVERTIME** badge with an upward counter. No hunting through menus to see where you are.

**Eating window tracking.** After you stop a fast, FastWasp immediately starts counting down your eating window. A wrist alert fires when it closes.

**OMAD mode.** Tracks elapsed time since your last meal with a thin progress bar toward the 23-hour target.

**Wrist alerts.** A wakeup is scheduled at your target time so the watch vibrates and shows a banner even if you close the app. Long pulse for fast complete, double pulse for eating window closed.

**Edit start time.** Forgot to tap START on time? Nudge the start by ±15 minutes without losing your streak.

**Stats.** Total fasts completed, average fast length, longest fast, and total overtime accumulated — all on one scrollable screen.

**100% offline.** All data lives in watch persistent storage. Nothing leaves your wrist.

Supports **Core 2 Duo** (`diorite`, 144×168 B&W) and **Pebble Time 2** (`emery`, 200×228 colour).
